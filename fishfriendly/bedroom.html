Cigarettes

Replace with: nothing. Stop smoking.

The number one item found on the clear-up was cigarettes/ cigarette filters. Over 2,117,000 were found in the coastal clear-up. They make their way through drains and gutters and find themselves in the ocean. Animals and birds can mistake the butts for food and ingest them, along with the toxins present in cigarettes. So you should stop smoking cigarettes the betterment of sea creatures, if not for your own health.
