Hello there, 

Welcome to FishFriendly!

This is an interactive desktop app which encourages you to replace everyday household items with ocean-friendly alternatives.

Save the turtles!

Here's why we built FishFriendly:


Ocean pollution affects ecosystems worldwide in a far more disastrous manner than any land-based pollution can. Since pollutants and rubbish in the ocean can travel far and wide, the more pollutants that are present in the ocean, the wider the spread, affecting everyone, right from sea creatures and plants, to people who rely on the ocean for their food and livelihood.

Often, people make it seem like the best way to contribute to stopping marine pollution is beach cleaning and other remedial measures, forgetting that there's no chance of stopping the tide of waste unless they take PREVENTIVE measures as well!

This is what FishFriendly aims to solve.

By preventing more waste from hitting our precious oceans, we are future-proofing ourselves from the impacts of pollution and climate change, and the only way to achieve this is en masse.
Use FishFriendly well, fellow human!
